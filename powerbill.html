Georgia Power Bill Calculator

The utility bill calculator is a resource that the Commission is making available that allows a consumer to check the accuracy of his/her monthly electric bill. A bill calculator has been prepared for customers of the Georgia Power Company. This bill calcualtor applies only to residential customers who are receiving service under the standard Residential Tariff (R-27). for more details regarding Georgia Power's residential tariff offerings, please visit Georgia Power Pricing & Rates.
