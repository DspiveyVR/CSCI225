  2. Contingency (truth table generator)

    When I took Discrete Math for Computer Science we had some homework assignments where we had to draw the truth table for a given logical expression (such as. p v ~q) and I did not want to go through the tedious process of calculating the truth tables myself so I wrote a program in Rust to do it for me. This probably didn't save me any time but it was fun.

  3. Incorporeal (melodic extratone song)

    Here's some of my weird music. This song's genre is called "melodic extratone." Extratone is an extreme subgenre of electronic music where distorted kickdrums are played at such a speed that it creates a tone. This effect can be heard at 3:05 in the song. The bassline that you hear is actually just kickdrums played at over 2000 bpm. This song is actually a pretty tame example of this genre. Extratone songs are usually more harsh and far less melodic. For an example of that you can listen to nausea, which is another one of my songs (that I released under a different alias) and it is heavily inspired by extreme forms of metal music.
